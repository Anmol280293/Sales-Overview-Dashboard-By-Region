# Sales Overview Dashboard | By Region

---

##  **1. Introduction**

“Hi, this dashboard is a *Sales Overview Dashboard by Region*. It provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across different regions, comparing current year (CY) metrics with previous year (PY), Year on Year (YoY) and highlighting growth trends, profitability, and quantity sold.”

---

##  **2. Business Problem**

“The business was facing challenges in understanding:

* Which regions are driving growth
* Where sales are declining or stagnant
* How profit and quantity trends align with revenue
  There was no single unified view to track regional performance efficiently.”

---

##  **3. Goal of the Dashboard**

“The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor **regional sales performance**
* Compare **year-over-year growth (YoY)**
* Identify **top-performing states and regions**
* Track **profitability and sales quantity trends**
* Enable quick, data-driven decision-making”

---

##  **4. Breakdown Framework**

“I structured the dashboard into 4 key sections:

1. **Regional Sales Trends (Left Panel)**
2. **Geographical Sales Distribution (Map)**
3. **State-Level Performance (Bar Chart)**
4. **CY vs PY Metrics Table (Bottom Section)**”

---

##  **5. Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

### 🔹 Regional Sales Trends (Left Side)

* Displays **Central, East, West, and South regions**
* Monthly sales trends shown using bar charts
* Includes **CY Sales vs PY Sales comparison**

---

###  Sales by State (Map + Bar Chart)

* Map visualizes **geographical spread of sales**
* Bubble size indicates sales volume
* Bar chart ranks **top-performing states**

---

###  Current Year (CY) vs Previous Year (PY) Metrics Table

* Displays:

  * CY Sales vs PY Sales
  * YoY Growth %
  * Profit comparison
  * Quantity sold

---

##  **6. Chart Description & Insights**

* **West Region**

  * Highest sales and profit growth
  * Strong upward trend → expansion opportunity

* **East Region**

  * Moderate growth (~18%)
  * Stable but needs optimization

* **Central Region**

  * Flat sales (0% growth)
  * Profit declined → efficiency issue

* **South Region**

  * Good sales growth (~31%)
  * Profit dropped significantly → cost concerns

---

##  **7. Key Insights**

* Revenue growth does not always mean profit growth
* West is the **most valuable region**
* Central and South need **cost optimization strategies**
* Sales are heavily concentrated in a few states → **risk of dependency**

---

##  **8. Business Impact**

“This dashboard helps stakeholders to:

* Identify **high-growth regions for investment**
* Detect **low-performing areas needing intervention**
* Optimize **profit margins**
* Improve **resource allocation and sales strategy**
